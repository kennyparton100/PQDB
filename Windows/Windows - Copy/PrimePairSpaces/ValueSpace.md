# Value Space (p, q)

**Axes:** Actual prime values.
**Coordinates:** `(p, q)` where `p` and `q` are primes.
**Product:** `N = p * q`

## Purpose

This is the honest geometry of factor pairs.

- **Near diagonal** => balanced semiprimes
- **Far from diagonal** => skewed semiprimes

"Small factor strip" and "near-square band" make actual sense in this space.

## Example (primes 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13)

```
          q ->
          2    3    5    7    11   13
p v
2         4    6   10   14    22   26
3         6    9   15   21    33   39
5        10   15   25   35    55   65
7        14   21   35   49    77   91
11       22   33   55   77   121  143
13       26   39   65   91   143  169
```

Same products as index space, but axes carry real magnitude information.
